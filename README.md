A centralized management platform for employment events, employer relations, and industry insights, serving the Jerusalem Employment Authority.

## Overview
The platform serves as a digital bridge between the Jerusalem Employment Authority, local coordinators, and employers. It centralizes event management, provides a secure employer directory with advanced privacy controls, and delivers automated professional content.

## Project Goals
The primary goal of this project is to centralize and streamline the interaction between the Jerusalem Employment Authority, local employers, and employment coordinators. By providing a unified digital portal, we aim to improve event management, data accessibility, and professional knowledge sharing while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy.

## The Three Main "Legs" of the Project

### 1. Annual Event Calendar 
A centralized hub for managing and displaying employment-related events. 
- **For Employers:** A clear view of upcoming workshops, job fairs, and networking events with easy registration.
- **For Coordinators:** Tools to create, schedule, and manage events effectively.

### 2. Employer & Coordinator Directory 
A comprehensive and secure database of contacts within the Jerusalem employment ecosystem.
- **Privacy First:** Features a "Double Opt-in" mechanism to protect personal contact information.
- **Controlled Access:** Role-based visibility ensures that sensitive data is only seen by authorized personnel.

### 3. Automated Content Bot 
A smart web scraping engine designed to keep the community informed.
- **Efficiency:** Automatically scans relevant websites for employment news and professional articles.
- **Human Oversight:** Scraped content remains in "Pending" status until reviewed and approved by a manager, ensuring quality and relevance.
